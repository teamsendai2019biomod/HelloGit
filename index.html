Cell Migration

TEAMSENDAI

Abstract




As patriotic nations living in the land of rising robotics, we wish we could make precise and locomoting mm sized robot, like the white blood cell and growth cone in human body. Recent study succeeded in making self-contained and controllable shape shifting Amoeba-Like Robot of liposome. However, because it is in a closed state, it cannot interact with the outside environment.

Here, we designed the outside-interacting soft robot of DNA hydrogel. In this system, we can control DNA hydrogel extension/ shrinkage at the specific spot depending on the outside environment condition. What is more, we propose the system that it can extend/ shrink in a reversible manner.

In the future, this may contribute to the development of highly regulatable and designable DNA soft robot(124 characters)（0808 22:17）
